Cocktails and Dreamsz

Cocktails & Dreams is a Catering Service Company that Specializes in the creation of signature beverages to give your event a fresh and innovative style to celebrate parties. This is the first and the coolest beverage catering service in Davao City. Cocktails & Dreams were established in the year 2007 and have been in the business for 12 years with DTI registration number. Since 2007 Cocktails & Dreams have been creating trendy cocktails bar crafting of truly delicious appealing drinks & exemplary international standard service for weddings, debut corporate events, grand opening, shower/ stag party first birthday even christening. We have the LOVE for a good cocktail, it's always freshly made (mixed on the spot) we always deliver on taste.

We have developed a reputation for efficiency and excellence. Cocktails and Dreams is owned & managed by an international bartender with experience & expertise in different world cruise ship bartending service like Five-Star Cruise Line (Costa Crociere) in European Countries through the Years. The knowledge, skillsand outlook towards its operation have been gained through fortitude.

At Cocktails and Dreams your event is very important to us so we ensure to provide a quality bar service with international standards. We follow standards comparable to 5-star hotels abroad & give our clients VIP treatment. We distinguished ourselves by offering our client simple & honest pricing and competitive rates. Our mission is to change the face of common events & house parties by extending exemplary service.

We have been to lucky to work with some prestigious clients (DuPont, Hyundai, Aeon, Concentrix, Thailand tourism, MiceCon 2013, Vital C, AMPC, SUBIC Clark, Davao Light-Aboitiz, RCBC, Water Front Insular Hotel)

We efficiently catered events all throughout Metro Davao & other parts of Mindanao. We are proud to be recognized as one of Mindanao's leading Mobile Bar. We are definitely the best in the Business we make sure clients will be satisfied up to the last drop.
